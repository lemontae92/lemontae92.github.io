About

The Black Box, also known as the flight data recorder, is the memory unit on an aircraft. It is used in fields such as aviation accident investigation, maintenance, and flight experiments. It symbolizes the preservation of memory and data. In contrast, the white box represents forgetting and liberation.

The white box symbolizes our pursuit of freedom in creativity, transcending traditional limitations, and breaking free from constraints.

Philosophy

  • Committed to breaking traditional constraints, creating an unconstrained space for artists.
  • Providing artists with a platform to showcase their individuality and free expression, encouraging them to explore creativity and imagination from within.

Future

  • White Box is a community of passionate artists, and we believe that every artist has the right to pursue true artistic freedom.
  • White Box will become a symbol of liberating artists’ thoughts and creative approaches, encouraging them to transcend traditional frameworks and strive for uniqueness and innovation.
  • Integrating cross-disciplinary innovation, leading avant-garde art, and building an open, diverse artistic space.
